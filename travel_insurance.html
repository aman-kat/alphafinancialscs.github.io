Travel Insurance
(make your journey safe)

Description

Travel insurance is an insurance policy that covers unexpected losses incurred while traveling overseas or domestic, losses can be minor, like loss of baggage, or prominent, like your flight gets cancelled on the last minute or a medical emergency occurs when you're overseas. Some countries require that you have a proper travel insurance policy as a condition of entry or granting a visa.

How it works?

When planning your next trip, make sure that you have a travel insurance policy, which will provide you with compensation in case of any unfortunate accidents occurred during the journey. Imagine you're somewhere overseas, and you'd an accident well it does not sound good. Besides, when it comes to uncertainties there is surely no end to it. In case you encounter such riskey situations, it can drain you financially, physically and emotionally, so buy yourself a good travel insurance policy.

Most policies covers
- Medical Expenses/evacuation
- Personal Accident
- Loss of Passport
- Theft/Loss of Checked-in Baggage
- Trip Cancellation or Interruptions

Types

Corporate Travel Insurance: These Insurance plans are designed for corporates, travelling for business purposes.
Domestic Travel Insurance: This type of insurance plan meant for people travelling within the geographical borders of India.
Family Travel Insurance: As the name says, it is for families travelling overseas together. It usually covers the policyholder, his/her spouse and his dependent children.
Flight Insurance: If case of any adversity due to the fault of the airline or their staff or any unforseen flight-related emergency, for these type of things there is a tailor-made insurance plan called Flight Insurance.
Group Travel Insurance: This insurance plan is meant for people travelling together in groups, like group tours, industrial training etc.
International Travel Insurance: This type of insurance plan covers individuals and families travelling overseas. It provides a wide range of coverage.
Medical Travel Insurance: These kind of insurance plans provides extensive medical coverage to travellers during their journey.
Multi-trip Travel Insurance: This insurance plan is for those people who travels overseas lots of time in a year.
Schengen Travel Insurance: This insurance policy covers people travelling to one or more than one Schengen Countries.
Senior Citizen Travel Insurance: These policies are tailor-made for travellers above the age of 60.
Single trip Travel Insurance: This insurance plan provides protection for one single journey.
Student Travel Insurance: As the name says, it is a insurance plan that has been customized for Indian students travelling overseas to get education.

Benefits

1) Emergency Hospitalization & Medical Treatment
2) Compensation for Flight-related Advensitites
3) Customized policies for Senior Citizens
4) Available for Frequent Flyers
5) Replacement of lost or stolen Baggage
6) Emergency Cash Assistance
7) Pays for personal liability
8) Cover for cancelled trip or trip delay
