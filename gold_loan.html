Gold Loan
(it's a good option in case of financial emergancy)

Description

Gold loan is a secured loan in which the borrower pledges his/her gold jewellery or gold coins as collateral with a gold loan company, you have to deposit your gold with the lender, you will get them back as soon as you pays back the loan amount inculuding interest. The financial institutes gives a loan amount as per the market value of gold to the borrower. Gold loan being secure, features short processing times, a low interest rate and short to medium term tenure. Unlike other secured loans such as a home loan or car loan, there are no restrictions on the end use of gold loans.

How does it work?

Gold loans are secured loans where gold jewellery is used as collateral. You pledge your gold jewellery with the lender, after evaluations of pledged gold's purity and weight, the lender sanctions the loan. After the repayment (principal amount + interest), you get back your gold jewellery, as simple as its possible. Nationalised banks, private banks, and other financial institutions offer these loans at affordable interest rates.

The gold’s market value is determined based on its current rate. Also, the approval of this loan does not depend upon your credit history or income. The process is quick and usually takes a day. So whether you need to fund a wedding, family vacation or your child’s education, it is a great way to meet your sudden money requirement. Most lenders offer a gold loan with a value up to 75 percent of the pledged gold’s market value. Gold loan tenures are usually short and range from to 3-12 months. However, some lenders may offer you a longer tenure and if gold prices increase drastically during the loan tenure, banks may ask for the extra payment.

Documentation

Photos: 2-3 Passport size photographs
Identity Proof: Aadhaar card, PAN card, Driver's license, Passport (in case, you don't have a PAN card, you'll be asked to submit Form 60)
Address Proof: Aadhaar card, Utility bills (electricity, telephone etc), Passport
- Some lenders might ask you for your income proof

Benefits

1) Anyone who is above 18 years old can apply for gold loan, obiviously as long as the person seeking a loan own the Gold.
2) It is a secured loan. Therefore, there is no requirement of a good credit history to avail a gold loan.
3) Gold Loans tenure, starts from a few days and goes upto 5 years (more depending upon the lender).
5) In most cases, there aren't any pre-payment penalty on gold loans.
6) Minimal documentation required.
7) Fast disbursement i.e. loan is provided within a few hours.
8) For those of you involved in agriculture, gold loan schemes can be availed at a reduced rate of interest which can be as low as 8%.
9) In case of urgency like funding a wedding, or your child's education, gold loan is good option.
10) Gold loans features quite low interest rates as compared to other loan options.

Consider these before applying for gold loan

- All financial institutions offer loan of up to 75% of the gold value, after checking the metal for purity and the market gold prices.

- While Gold Jewelry is perfectly acceptable to any bank or NBFC as collateral, Gold Bars are not accepted and only specific types of Gold Coins are accepted as collateral by banks only.

- If the ornaments pledged feature precious stones, they are valued only on the basis of the weight of gold in the ornaments.

- If you do not pay back the loan amount in given time, the pledged gold can be seized by the bank.

So, what's the upshot?

Well, gold loans are really good if you have an urgent financial requirement and you do have gold. But if you feel like you can not return the money with interest in time, then you should avoid Gold loans.
