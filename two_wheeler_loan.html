Two Wheeler Loan
(you can also avail for super bikes)

Description

A two-wheeler loan helps you purchase a motorcycle or a scooter of your choice and pay for it in EMIs. Both banks as well as NBFCs offer these loans. Two-wheeler loans are available for both salaried and self-employed individuals. However, to qualify for a loan from these lenders, you need to fulfil certain eligibility criteria.

How it works?

Two wheeler loans are considered to be the simplest and the fastest way to buy your dream bike. A two wheeler loan also known as a bike loan requires you to pay a minimum down payment up front, this increases your loan-to-value (LTV) ratio. Mostly banks and NBFCs offer financing up to 90% of the vehicle amount, so you only have to pay the 10% as down payment.

It works as all other loans, banks/financial institution offers you loan to buy your dream bike and you pay the loan amount (principal amount) in given peroid with interest. There is no such fixed credit score required for a bike loan, but it is advisable to have a score between 600 and 750, as this range is considered ideal for banks and credit institutions. Once your loan application has been approved you can ride out with a two-wheeler of your choice.

1Eligibilty Criteria

- Age: 21 years to 65 years
- Applicant must be a salaired or self-employed
- Income norms: 25K/per month or above
- Area of Residence (If you're living in a metro city there are better chances of getting a loan)

Benefits

1) You can get almost all the funds you need to buy the bike you want
2) You can also get funds to buy superbikes as well
3) You will get affordable interest rates
4) Wide spectrum of loan repayment periods available

So, what's the upshot?

If you're looking to buy a bike you should always consider taking bike loans, as for some men's their bike is something else in their life and bike loans helps them buy the dream bike, without disturbing their planned budget as you'll for it in EMIs. There are loans for Super Bikes also, so taking a bike loan is always good, if you're looking to buy your dream bike.
